Grading

  • Homework: 10%
  • Peer Grading: 10%
    • 4% for completing the peer reviews.
    • 2% for completing the rating.
    • 4% for quality of the reviews. This is subjective, but we will glance at the comments you provided, and take into account students' ratings of your comments and the accuracy of your scores. To measure accuracy, we will fit a statistical model to the peer grades at the end of the quarter.
  • Quizzes: 40%
    • We don't want to foster competition, so the grades will not be "curved". (That is, if everyone does well, then everyone can earn an A.)
    • However, the grades will be adjusted upwards as follows: we will determine a mastery score $M$ for each quiz. This score is guaranteed to earn you a solid A (not an A-). The mastery score for the quizzes are:
      • Quiz 1: $M=32$ out of 36
      • Quiz 2: $M=35$ out of 42
      • Quiz 3: $M=30$ out of 42
      • Quiz 4: $M\approx 32$ out of 36
      • Quiz 5: $M\approx 32$ out of 36
      If your score was $S$, then your adjusted score is computed as: $$\text{adjusted score} = \sqrt{0.9 \cdot \frac{S}{M}} \times 100\%.$$ Typically, an adjusted score of 87% or higher is some form of A, and an adjusted score of 70% of higher is some form of B, but the exact cutoffs will be determined at the end of the quarter.
  • Final: 40%
